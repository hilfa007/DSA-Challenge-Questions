Two Number Sum

Problem statement:-
- Given an array of integers, and a target sum number, return an array of elements that add up to that target sum number.
- If there is no such pair, return -1.

Constraint:- 
- You cannot just add a single number to itself to make the sum.

Input format:-
2 7 6 4 5
10

Output format:-
6 4

Test Cases:-
1) Input :
     4 6 7 8 5 9
     10
    Output :
    4 6

2) Input :
    6 6 6 6 6 6 
    10
    Output :
    -1
